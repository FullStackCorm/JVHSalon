OUR TEAM

MEET OUR TEAM OF SUPERIOR STYLISTS

We believe ongoing education and exploration are key to keeping up with current trends. Each member of our staff is given stipend funds and time away from the salon to pursue this goal - because investing in our team is an investment for our guests.

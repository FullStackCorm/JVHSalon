OUR STORY

Our mission is to help individuals of any age to discover their best traits and achieve the level of self-confidence they deserve.

J&H Vanity Salon is a full-service hair salon serving residents of Oak Harbor, Port Clinton and their surrounding areas since 2003. Our founders grew up in Ottawa County and were struck by the lack of modern salon offerings for their communities upon their return - so they did something about it.

Trained and apprenticed in Los Angeles, Chicago, and New York City, our founders returned to their Ohio homelands to fulfill an unmet desire for high quality, uniquely artistic contemporary hair styling in Ottawa County.
